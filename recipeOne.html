French Toast



Recipe

Brioche Bread
3 Eggs
1 Egg Yolk
1 cup Whole Milk
1 1/2 teaspoon Cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon of Nutmeg
3 tablespoons Milk
1/4 teaspoon Salt
1 tablespoon Dark Brown Sugar












Directions

  1. Whisk eggs, egg yolk, dark brown sugar, nutmeg, cinnamon, and salt in a shallow dish like a pie plate.
  2. Slowly add the milk into the egg mixture.
  3. Cut brioche into about 2 inch slices.
  4. Heat up a pan over medium heat and add in the butter.
  5. Then, dunk one side of the bread in for a few seconds, flip, and dunk the other side of the bread for a few more seconds.
  6. Place the bread onto the heated pan and let it cook for about 3 minutes. Then, flip and cook for 3 minutes on the other side.
  7. Repeat with the rest of the bread.
  8. Plate however you would like! Add some fresh fruit, yougurt, and top with orange zest for a refreshing breakfast. Or add some whipped cream and syrup for a classic French Toast. Be creative and add whatever you would like!
